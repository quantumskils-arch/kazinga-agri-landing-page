Who We Are

A Savings Group That Builds Real Businesses

Kazinga Agri is a Village Savings and Loan Association (VSLA) based in Gayaza-Kasangati, Wakiso District. We combine community savings with practical agri-business skills so members can turn small backyards into reliable income.

  • Save together and access affordable loans
  • Learn proven, low-cost farming methods
  • Sell digital guides, courses and farm products
  • Get ongoing mentorship from fellow members
