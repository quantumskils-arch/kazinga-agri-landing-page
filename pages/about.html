About Kazinga Agri

A community savings group turning backyards into businesses in Gayaza-Kasangati, Wakiso District.

Our Story

From Saving Together to Building Together

Kazinga Agri Entrepreneurship Initiative began as a small Village Savings and Loan Association (VSLA) of neighbours in Gayaza-Kasangati who wanted more than a savings box — they wanted skills and income. By pooling savings and sharing knowledge, members started funding real home-based farming ventures.

Today we bring together over 120 members who save weekly, access affordable loans, and train one another in eight proven agri-businesses. Our focus is simple: practical, low-cost activities that any household can run from home and grow into a steady income.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower families in Wakiso with the savings, skills and support they need to build sustainable home-based income.

🌅

Our Vision

A community where every household has a thriving backyard enterprise and financial freedom within reach.

🤝

Our Values

Community first, sustainability, honesty in savings, and learning by doing — together.

How VSLA Works

Save, Borrow, Grow

  • 💰 Weekly savings — members contribute a small, agreed amount each week.
  • 🏦 Affordable loans — borrow from the pooled fund to start or expand a business.
  • 📈 Shared profits — interest returns to members at the end of each cycle.
  • 🎓 Free training — every member can learn any of our 8 activities.
