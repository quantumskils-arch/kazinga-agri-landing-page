8 Income-Generating Activities

Practical, low-cost businesses you can run from home — with training, starter guidance and ongoing support.

🐝 Beekeeping

Keep bees for honey, wax and propolis. Hives need little space and only occasional attention, making it ideal as a side income.

Startup: ~UGX 250kIncome: UGX 150k+/moProfit in: 4–6 mo
Enrol for training

🐔 Poultry Farming

Raise layers for eggs or broilers for meat. Fast-moving demand and quick cycles make poultry a dependable earner.

Startup: ~UGX 400kIncome: UGX 200k+/moProfit in: 2–3 mo
Enrol for training

🍄 Oyster Mushroom Farming

Grow high-value oyster mushrooms in a dark room or shed. They mature in weeks and sell quickly at markets and to restaurants.

Startup: ~UGX 150kIncome: UGX 120k+/moProfit in: 1 mo
Enrol for training

🪰 BSF Farming

Farm Black Soldier Fly larvae to turn kitchen waste into cheap, protein-rich feed for poultry and pigs — cutting your feed bills.

Startup: ~UGX 120kSaves: feed costsProfit in: 1–2 mo
Enrol for training

☕ Coffee Processing

Add value to coffee by drying, hulling and roasting before selling. Processed coffee fetches far higher prices than raw cherries.

Startup: ~UGX 300kIncome: UGX 250k+/moProfit in: seasonal
Enrol for training

🌿 Urban Farming

Grow vegetables in sacks, containers and small plots. Perfect for tight city spaces and a steady supply of fresh greens to sell.

Startup: ~UGX 80kIncome: UGX 100k+/moProfit in: 1–2 mo
Enrol for training

🐷 Piggery

Pigs grow fast and have strong market demand. With good feeding and care, a small piggery becomes a high-return business.

Startup: ~UGX 500kIncome: UGX 300k+/cycleProfit in: 5–6 mo
Enrol for training
